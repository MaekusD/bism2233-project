History


It all started when a group of uni students, like yourselves, were sick and tired of the hassle of public transportation.
They thought that there must have been another way to get to and from campus.
They decided to take advantage of the personal transportation of existing students.
To create something for students, by students.


What We Offer


UNIDRIVES proposes a possible means of commuting to campus for university students.
We provide a platform that allows students to share their ride details online.
Students will be able to carpool to campus with other fellow students at a cost similar to public transport.
Not only is it a means of transportation, UNIDRIVES is also an application that promotes social interaction!
