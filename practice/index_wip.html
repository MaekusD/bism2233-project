The Better Way of Getting to University

Why worry about missing the bus, train or ferry? UNIDRIVES provides the solution to every student's transportation needs.

Features

UNIDRIVES goal is to help university students manage their financial life, through providing solutions that help save money on transportation.

Book rides with confidence without hurting your wallet.

UNIDRIVES provides a cheaper alternative compared to other ridesharing platforms through considering student budgets.

Compensate petrol each time you pick up a fellow rider.

Why pay for petrol alone? Receive money to cover your gas expenses everytime you carpool with another rider.

Meet new people and find friends along the way!

UNIDRIVES is not only a ridesharing platform, it is a social experience that connects students together.
